# Myers-Briggs Personality Types (MBTI)

---

## 1. **ISTJ – The Logistician**
- **Traits:** Responsible, detail-oriented, practical, organized, reliable.  
- **Strengths:** Strong work ethic, logical, dependable, methodical.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be stubborn, inflexible, overly focused on rules.  
- **Career Fit:** Accountant, Auditor, Lawyer, Project Manager, Police Officer.

---

## 2. **ISFJ – The Defender**
- **Traits:** Caring, loyal, meticulous, observant, practical.  
- **Strengths:** Compassionate, dependable, detail-focused, patient.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be overly selfless, avoid confrontation, resistant to change.  
- **Career Fit:** Nurse, Teacher, Social Worker, Administrator, Librarian.

---

## 3. **INFJ – The Advocate**
- **Traits:** Insightful, idealistic, creative, sensitive, determined.  
- **Strengths:** Visionary, empathetic, inspiring, determined.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be perfectionistic, overly idealistic, private.  
- **Career Fit:** Psychologist, Writer, Counselor, Teacher, Humanitarian.

---

## 4. **INTJ – The Architect**
- **Traits:** Strategic, independent, analytical, logical, ambitious.  
- **Strengths:** Innovative, decisive, long-term thinker, confident.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be perfectionistic, dismissive of emotions, stubborn.  
- **Career Fit:** Scientist, Engineer, Strategist, Researcher, IT Manager.

---

## 5. **ISTP – The Virtuoso**
- **Traits:** Adventurous, practical, spontaneous, logical, action-oriented.  
- **Strengths:** Problem solver, adaptable, hands-on, independent.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be impatient, risk-prone, private, avoid long-term planning.  
- **Career Fit:** Engineer, Pilot, Mechanic, Detective, Emergency Responder.

---

## 6. **ISFP – The Adventurer**
- **Traits:** Gentle, sensitive, artistic, flexible, easygoing.  
- **Strengths:** Creative, compassionate, adaptable, observant.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be overly reserved, avoid conflict, indecisive.  
- **Career Fit:** Artist, Designer, Chef, Nurse, Photographer.

---

## 7. **INFP – The Mediator**
- **Traits:** Idealistic, empathetic, creative, introspective, loyal.  
- **Strengths:** Deeply caring, imaginative, value-driven, flexible.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be overly sensitive, unrealistic, avoid practical matters.  
- **Career Fit:** Writer, Counselor, Psychologist, Artist, Humanitarian.

---

## 8. **INTP – The Thinker**
- **Traits:** Analytical, logical, curious, independent, innovative.  
- **Strengths:** Objective, inventive, critical thinker, theoretical.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be absent-minded, indecisive, socially detached.  
- **Career Fit:** Scientist, Programmer, Philosopher, Researcher, Mathematician.

---

## 9. **ESTP – The Entrepreneur**
- **Traits:** Energetic, outgoing, practical, action-oriented, perceptive.  
- **Strengths:** Bold, adaptable, persuasive, risk-taker, problem-solver.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be impulsive, impatient, prone to boredom.  
- **Career Fit:** Sales Manager, Entrepreneur, Athlete, Pilot, Detective.

---

## 10. **ESFP – The Entertainer**
- **Traits:** Sociable, fun-loving, spontaneous, energetic, friendly.  
- **Strengths:** Outgoing, adaptable, resourceful, empathetic, engaging.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be impulsive, disorganized, avoid long-term planning.  
- **Career Fit:** Actor, Event Planner, Teacher, Social Worker, Performer.

---

## 11. **ENFP – The Campaigner**
- **Traits:** Enthusiastic, imaginative, sociable, creative, spontaneous.  
- **Strengths:** Inspirational, energetic, adaptable, empathetic.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be scattered, impulsive, overly idealistic.  
- **Career Fit:** Writer, Entrepreneur, Coach, Marketer, Teacher.

---

## 12. **ENTP – The Debater**
- **Traits:** Innovative, witty, energetic, curious, bold.  
- **Strengths:** Strategic, quick-thinking, persuasive, inventive.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be argumentative, impatient, easily bored.  
- **Career Fit:** Entrepreneur, Lawyer, Consultant, Inventor, Public Speaker.

---

## 13. **ESTJ – The Executive**
- **Traits:** Organized, practical, logical, responsible, assertive.  
- **Strengths:** Efficient, dependable, structured, clear communicator.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be inflexible, overly critical, stubborn.  
- **Career Fit:** Manager, Judge, Officer, Administrator, Project Lead.

---

## 14. **ESFJ – The Consul**
- **Traits:** Warm, caring, sociable, conscientious, organized.  
- **Strengths:** Supportive, cooperative, loyal, empathetic, team-oriented.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be sensitive to criticism, people-pleasing, anxious.  
- **Career Fit:** Teacher, Nurse, Event Planner, Social Worker, Customer Service.

---

## 15. **ENFJ – The Protagonist**
- **Traits:** Charismatic, empathetic, persuasive, idealistic, organized.  
- **Strengths:** Inspiring, supportive, empathetic, good leaders.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be overly selfless, struggle with criticism, perfectionistic.  
- **Career Fit:** Teacher, Counselor, Leader, Politician, Coach.

---

## 16. **ENTJ – The Commander**
- **Traits:** Bold, strategic, efficient, confident, natural leader.  
- **Strengths:** Decisive, goal-oriented, confident, visionary.  
- **Weaknesses:** Can be domineering, impatient, insensitive to emotions.  
- **Career Fit:** CEO, Executive, Lawyer, Entrepreneur, Manager.
